How to play

Your ball orbits a ring. Tap to fling it to the next ring — aim the gold line at the ring's center. Hit dead-center to build your combo (×2, ×3 … up to ×10). A miss resets the streak. The longer your chain, the higher your score.

Common questions

How do I pause? Tap the pause button (top-right) during a game.

How do I mute sound? Tap the speaker button (top-right), or toggle Sound in the pause menu.

Why do I see ads? Ads keep Sling free. They appear briefly between some rounds.
