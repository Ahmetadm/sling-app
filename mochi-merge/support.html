How to play

Drop a mochi into the jar. When two of the same mochi touch, they merge into a bigger, cuter one. Keep merging to grow them from tiny to giant and raise your score — just don't let the jar overflow past the top line.

Common questions

How do I pause? Tap the pause button (top-right) during a game.

How do I mute sound? Tap the speaker button (top-right), or toggle Sound in the pause menu.

Is my best score saved? Yes — it's stored on your device and shown in-game and on the pause screen.

Why do I see ads? Ads keep Mochi Merge free. They appear briefly between some rounds and on the pause screen.
