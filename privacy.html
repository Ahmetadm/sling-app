Privacy Policy

Sling · Last updated 14 June 2026

Sling ("the app", "we") is a casual arcade game. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

We do not ask you for any personal information, accounts, or sign-in.

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google's advertising SDK may collect and process:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA / Ad ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Approximate (coarse) location, derived from your IP address
  • Ad interaction and in-app usage data (ads shown, taps, gameplay interactions)

This data is used to deliver and measure advertising. On iOS the app asks for your permission (App Tracking Transparency) before using the advertising identifier for personalized ads; if you decline, you still see ads, but non-personalized ones.

We (the developer) do not collect, store, or sell your personal data ourselves, and we do not see your identity.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads — see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data.

Children

Sling is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and the in-app prompt.
  • You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
